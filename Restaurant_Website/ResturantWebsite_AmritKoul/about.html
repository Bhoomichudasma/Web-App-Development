ABOUT US

Our Story Casa di Sapori was born out of a passion for Italian cuisine and a desire to bring its authentic taste to India. Our founders, Amrit Koul and Anmol Koul , have always been enchanted by the rustic charm and culinary artistry of Italy. After years of exploring the diverse regions of Italy, they decided to create a place where guests could enjoy traditional Italian dishes made with the finest ingredients.
Our Chef Under the expert guidance of our head chef, Alok Singh, who trained in Italy's finest culinary schools, our kitchen brings traditional recipes to life with a contemporary twist. Chef Alok's dedication to culinary excellence ensures that every dish not only meets but exceeds our guests' expectations.
